Welcome to The Roost

The Roost TCG is a welcoming space for collectors, competitive players, and families who enjoy trading card games. Whether you are looking for weekly events, featured products, or help getting started, our goal is to build a strong and fun local community.

Visit the official Play Pokémon page to learn more about organized play opportunities.

Upcoming Events

Stay up to date on weekly league nights, casual play sessions, and tournament weekends. Our events page will help players quickly find dates, times, and format details.

Featured Products

The shop section will highlight featured products, accessories, and seasonal releases. This gives visitors a quick snapshot of what is new and what is popular in the store.

Community Support

New players and returning players can find guides, resources, and store information that make it easier to join the community and participate in local events.

Why The Roost?

  • Friendly environment for both casual and competitive players
  • Organized events and league play
  • Support for new players and families
  • Local community resources and store updates
