Events and League Play

Events are one of the main ways The Roost TCG supports the local player community. This page highlights the types of gatherings visitors can expect as the shop grows.

Weekly League Night

Casual play for new and experienced players, with a focus on learning, trading, and community building.

Tournament Weekends

Structured play events for players who enjoy testing decks and competing in a friendly environment.

New Player Sessions

Beginner-focused sessions that help players understand rules, deck basics, and event etiquette.
